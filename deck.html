✦ AI Today
The portfolio, summarised before anyone logs in.
Every morning the assistant reads the whole operation — arrivals, departures, flagged issues, reviews — and turns it into one proactive briefing, not a wall of emails to triage.
The feature
•In-stay, check-in & check-out counts at a glance, on the dashboard and in the assistant drawer
•"Things that need you today" ranked by urgency — a stuck work order, a low review, an overdue invoice
•One tap from the summary straight into the screen that fixes it
Business benefit
Nobody starts the day guessing what's on fire.
Replaces the morning scroll through inboxes and spreadsheets with a 10-second read — freeing coordinators to act, not triage.
✦ Talk to AI
One tap from an answer — on every screen.

The feature
•Understands the whole portfolio, not just whatever screen is open
•Drafts a reply, pulls a performance number, explains a policy — in one message
•No menus to dig through — the answer comes to whichever screen you're on
Business benefit
Seconds to an answer, not a search.
Cuts the back-and-forth — between team members, and between team and system — that eats a coordinator's day.

The feature

Business benefit
